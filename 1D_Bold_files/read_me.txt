The 1D files are averaged time courses at different brain regions after pre-processing. Readers can refer to the article (Zhe Wang, Ahmed Alahmadi, David C. Zhu and Tongtong Li, "Causality Analysis of fMRI Data Based on The Directed Information Theory Framework", IEEE Transactions on Biomedical Engineering, Vol. 63, No. 5, 2016, pages: 1002-1015.) regarding the experiment design, data collection, and fMRI pre-processing.
